Cut & Stack Sequence Generator

This free tool solves a common problem in print production: creating properly sequenced numbered documents for N-up printing with cut-and-stack finishing.

The Problem:

When printing numbered items (tickets, forms, labels) multiple-up (2-up, 4-up, etc.), standard imposition results in non-sequential numbering after cutting and stacking. For example, printing tickets 1-100 at 4-up normally produces jumbled numbers when cut and stacked.

The Solution:

This tool generates the correct sequence order for your mail merge data file. Simply enter your starting number, ending number, and N-up layout, and it outputs a CSV or TXT file with numbers arranged so that after printing, cutting, and stacking, your finished pieces end up in perfect sequential order.

Perfect for:

  • Event tickets
  • Raffle tickets
  • Numbered forms
  • Sequential labels
  • Any print job requiring numbered items in order after cut-and-stack finishing

Features:

  • Supports any N-up configuration (2-up, 4-up, 6-up, etc.)
  • Optional leading zeros (0001, 0002, etc.)
  • Exports to CSV or TXT for mail merge
  • Works directly in your browser - no software to install

How to use:

  1. Download the CSV or TXT file
  2. Use this file as your data source for mail merge
  3. Print the merged document with -up tiling
  4. Cut each sheet into pieces
  5. Stack all pieces from position 1, then position 2, etc.
  6. Your tickets will now be in sequential order!
